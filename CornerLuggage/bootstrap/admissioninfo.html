Admissions Information

Welcome to School Name! We are thrilled that you are considering us for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to ensure that we admit students who will thrive academically, socially, and emotionally in our school community.

Application Process

To apply to School Name, please complete the online admissions form on our website. In addition to the form, we require the following documents:

  • Transcripts from the student's current school
  • Two letters of recommendation from teachers, coaches, or other adults who know the student well
  • A personal statement from the student explaining why they want to attend School Name

Once we have received all of the required documents, our admissions committee will review the application and make a decision about whether to admit the student. We strive to make admissions decisions within two weeks of receiving a complete application.

Tuition and Financial Aid

Our tuition for the 2023-2024 school year is $20,000 per student. We understand that the cost of tuition can be a significant financial burden for families, which is why we offer a limited amount of financial aid to qualifying students. To apply for financial aid, please complete the financial aid form on our website.

Open Houses and Tours

We invite prospective students and their families to attend one of our open houses or schedule a tour of our campus. During these events, you will have the opportunity to meet our faculty and staff, tour our facilities, and learn more about our academic programs and extracurricular activities. Please visit our Events page for upcoming open house dates and to schedule a campus tour.
